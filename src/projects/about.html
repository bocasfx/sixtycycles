SixtyCycles

Sixty Cycles is a collection of all the independent work that I've done throughout the years. This collection includes projects that I've done for other people as well as for my own interest. These projects are varied in nature but at the same time are all somehow interconnected. All of them are licensed under the Creative Commons - Attribution Share Alike license.

When I'm not glued to a computer screen or trying out new-old recipes in the kitchen, I spend most of my time having fun with my wife and daughter and playing bass with my psychedelic funk band The Sofa Kings.
